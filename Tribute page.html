Mother Teresa

An Albanian-born Indian Roman Catholic missionary and nun who devoted her life to helping those most in need

Here's a timeline of Mother Teresa's life

  • 1910 Birth
  • 1922 First Call to Religious Life
  • 1928 Became a Roman Catholic Nun (Sister Teresa)
  • 1929 Becomes a Teacher After Moving to Kolkata
  • 1937 Takes Final Vows as a Nun, Becomes Mother Teresa
  • 1946 Call to serve the poor
  • 1950 Founded Missionaries of Charity
  • 1957 Begins Work with Lepers
  • 1971 Receives Popes Peace Prize
  • 1979 Wins Nobel Peace Prize
  • 1980 Awarded with the Bharat Ratna
  • 1982 Rescues 37 Disabled Children
  • 1985 Receives Medal of Freedom
  • 1997 Death
